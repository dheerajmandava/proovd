About ProovdPulse

ProovdPulse is a real-time website visitor tracking widget that shows how many people are actively viewing your website and tracks engagement metrics.

Engagement Metrics

ProovdPulse tracks the following metrics:

  • Active users
  • Click count
  • Scroll percentage
  • Time on page
